My UX Skills

Figma

I use Figma to develop low- and high-fidelity prototypes, on Desktop, Mobile, and Wearables.

User Interviewing

I empathize emotionally with the user. I help them feel heard and understood by repeating what they said using my own words. I gently assure them there are no wrong answers.

Presentation

When presenting work, I excel at using a conversational style that helps the audience feel personally engaged with. I make sure that the farther person in the room can hear me, and I assume that not everyone in the audience will fully understand UX/UI/Developer vocabulary.
